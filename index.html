PhD Synopsis By Kaspar Kennady

Title: Automation of Retail Banking using Artificial Intelligence (AI) Image processing

Abstract

In today’s fast-paced digital landscape, businesses constantly seek innovative ways to enhance efficiency, streamline operations, and provide seamless customer experiences. The financial sector is no exception, and one of the key areas where automation and advanced technologies have significantly impacted is the loan origination process. Traditional loan origination methods often involve cumbersome paperwork, lengthy processing times, and potential bottlenecks.

With the advent of Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML), financial institutions are revolutionizing how loans are initiated, processed, and approved. This blog aims to delve into the transformative power of AI and ML in automating the loan origination process. We will explore the various stages of loan origination and highlight how intelligent algorithms and data-driven insights are reshaping each step to deliver faster, more accurate, and more efficient outcomes

AI-API’s, such as optical character recognition (OCR), natural language processing (NLP), and image analysis, can automate up to 90% of manual loan application processing tasks

AI image processing works through a combination of advanced algorithms, neural networks.

Introduction

The proliferation of digital financial services necessitates the creation of tools that technically robust and faster loan disbursement. Traditional Retail banking manual data entry and document upload complexity and lack of domain-specific constructs. AI advanced algorithm API seeks to bridge this gap by offering a Automate Loan processing quickly by extracting data from different sources by using image processing and calculating eligibility and disbursement of loan.

Objectives

  1. Design and Development: To design the AI image processing for retail banking loan origination system in the existing architecture to incorporating new AI API image processing for faster loan processing and disbursement of loan (PL/AL/HL etc).

    Automating Manual Tasks: AI-API’s, such as optical character recognition (OCR), natural language processing (NLP), and image analysis, can automate up to 90% of manual loan application processing tasks These technologies help improve workflow speed and accuracy by handling tasks like document management, data extraction, and validation

    Faster Loan Application Processing: By leveraging AI, mortgage lenders can achieve 2x to 25x faster capture and processing of both digital and paper loan applications. Robotic Process Automation (RPA), machine learning (ML), and intelligent image analysis contribute to streamlining the loan origination process

    .
  2. Implementation: To implement a AI image processing for retail banking loan origination system, ensuring compatibility with existing Retail banking system and data formats.

    Data Collection and Preprocessing The process begins with collecting a large dataset of labelled images relevant to the task, such as object recognition or image classification. The images are pre-processed, which may involve resizing, normalization, and data augmentation to ensure consistency and improve model performance.

    Feature Extraction Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs), a type of deep learning architecture, are commonly used for AI image processing. CNNs automatically learn and extract hierarchical features from images. They consist of layers with learnable filters (kernels) that detect patterns like edges, textures, and more complex features.

    Model Training The pre-processed images are fed into the CNN model for training. During training, the model adjusts its internal weights and biases based on the differences between its predictions and the actual labels in the training data. Backpropagation and optimization algorithms (e.g., stochastic gradient descent) are used to iteratively update the model's parameters to minimize prediction errors. The study will focus on the design, implementation, and evaluation of retail banking image processing, demonstrating its effectiveness through practical applications and case studies

    .
  3. Evaluation: To evaluate the effectiveness of AI Image processing through case studies, user feedback, and performance metrics.
  4. Documentation: To provide comprehensive documentation and tutorials for end-users, facilitating widespread adoption.

Literature Review

The literature review will cover the following areas:

  • Retail banking Domain- Loan origination system(LOS): Overview of LOS, their advantages, and successful implementations in various stages/fields.
  • Existing Loan origination processes: Examination of existing functionality and tools used in banking and NBFC.
  • Retail banking Principles: Study of retail banking and cognitive principles relevant to designing the application for the business.
  • Cultural Context in Software Development: Exploration of the role Artificial intelligence Image processing to populate the data from image automatically through AI adoption and effectiveness of software tools to increase the turnaround time to disbursement of loan.

Research Methodology

  1. Requirement Analysis: Conduct surveys and interviews with the borrower and lending Bank/NBFC FinTech professionals to gather requirements and identify pain points.
  2. AI Image processing Design: Change architectural Design (HLD/LLD) in the existing Loan origination system product to utilize or call newly developed AI Image processing API, to store trained data and compare the images and extract the labeled data from images for automated processing.
  3. Development of Image processing API: Implement AI Image processing API, to store trained data , compare the images , extract the labeled data from images for automated processing code into executable instructions, ensuring error handling and optimization.
  4. Case Studies: Apply Image processing API to real-world financial scenarios, documenting its impact on efficiency and accuracy.
  5. User Feedback: Collect and analyze feedback from users to refine features and improve training data.
  6. Performance Evaluation: Measure the performance of Retail banking Loan origination system disbursement in terms of execution speed, resource utilization, and error rates, Lenders ROI and borrower feedback.

Expected Outcomes

  • Faster Approvals: AI accelerates the review and approval of loan applications, benefiting both lenders and borrowers.
  • Improved Accuracy: AI-driven credit decisioning ensures more accurate assessments.
  • Enhanced Borrower Experience: Younger consumers expect technology-supported interactions and fast turnaround times, which AI can deliver.
  • AI is poised to significantly change the mortgage industry by making loan origination more efficient and cost-effective. Lenders are increasingly adopting AI technologies to stay competitive and provide better services.
  • Comprehensive documentation and case studies demonstrating the practical benefits of AI image processing.

Conclusion

The development of AI image processing represents a significant step towards making retail banking loan origination system accessible to a broader audience, particularly in the financial sector. By leveraging the familiarity of the Retail banking automation using AI image processing aims to empower Bank and NBFC with a tool that enhances productivity and accuracy, ultimately contributing to the digital transformation of the industry.
